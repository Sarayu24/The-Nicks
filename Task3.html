CELEBRATING 20 YEARS OF SILVER SIDE UP

“It’s hard to believe that Silver Side Up is 20 years old! For us, the album is filled with so many memories and moments that will last a lifetime....
Rock & Roll
Nickelback’s rise to become one of the most successful rock bands of the 2000s hit overdrive 20 years ago this month when the Canadian rockers released their breakout album, Silver Side Up. It quickly became a global phenomenon, selling more than 10 million copies worldwide while achieving multi-platinum certification in Canada (8x), the U.S. (6x), the U.K. (3x), and Australia (2x). The album’s lead single, “How You Remind Me,” was the catalyst. The anthem topped the charts in multiple countries, including the U.S., where Billboard ranked it as the top single of 2002. Nielsen SoundScan named it the most played song of the decade on U.S. radio, where it garnered more than 1.2 million spins between 2001 and the end of 2009. Today, the band’s award-winning song has tallied over 1.2 billion global streams and is on the brink of being certified 4x platinum.
